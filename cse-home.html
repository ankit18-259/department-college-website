About Us
In the year 1998, the department of Computer Science & Engineering was introduced with an intake capacity of 20 students. The department has well- structured curriculum and adequate laboratory resources. Apart from these, students also utilize the various facilities of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering department. Despite of the limited number of academic staff the department has completed a glorious decade. The placement scenario of the department has been very impressive. The students are well placed in various national and multinational majors.
Our Missions
  • Mission 1: To teach the students to use computers creatively and effectively as a versatile humanistic discipline of problem solving.
  • Mission 2: To provide up-to-date curricula needed for professional and academic goals of students.
  • Mission 3: To give students with experimental learning opportunities.
  • Mission 4: To teach the students to acquire new knowledge independently in a world that changes very promptly.
PEO
  • PEO 1: The graduates will be able to enhance their skills and productivity by working in a group or as an individual.
  • PEO 2: The graduates will be able to adapt themselves with the changing tools and technologies which will help them to design and deploy software and hardware to meet the needs of society.
  • PEO 3: With the skills and motivations, the graduates will be able to pursue academics and Research & Development.
PSO
  • PSO 1: To understand the core areas of Computer Science and Engineering that prepare them to face challenges and empower to solve them for the benefit of the mankind.
  • PSO 2: To acquire analytical, logical and programming skills which help to gain acumen in problem understanding and solving abilities in the different fields of science and engineering where computer use and support are intensely necessary.
  • PSO 2: To produce skilled manpower like engineers, academics, researchers and entrepreneurs to contribute to the society in a positive way. And also by extending best practises of ethical and moral obligations towards the social and environmental well being.
