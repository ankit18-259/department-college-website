About
The Assam Engineering College, the edifice that stands tall with dignity because of the plethora of engineers that has graduated from its mainspring, since its foundation in 1955, has been the premier institute in North East, regarding this field and has been celebrated for the same.The college is situated away from the clamor of the capital city Guwahati, and is nestled in the lap of nature surrounded by hills and colorful flame trees extended to several acres of land.
